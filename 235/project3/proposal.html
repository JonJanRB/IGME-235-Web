Time Fling

High Concept

Manipulate time and fling yourself upwards away from danger while avoiding dangerous triangles and collecting flings from circles.

Genre

Arcade

Platform

Both Desktop and Mobile

Story

Purely abstract. The player motivation is to not die from the white wave coming from below.

Aesthetics

Graphics

Abstract and simple polygons

Sound

Retro, using BFXR

Sound Effects

  • Menu Back
  • Death
  • Fling
  • Hitting Fling Orb
  • Menu Select
  • Hitting Spike
  • Menu Hover
  • Time Stop / Ready Fling

Gameplay

Mechanics

The player can choose when to stop time and fling, where to aim, how long to hold their time stop, and the intensity to fling themselves.

Control

  • Mouse down and drag
  • Mobile tap and drag

The player drags like a sling shot away from the desired direction and then lets go to fling themselves.

Teaching

In order to start playing once you get into the game, you are required to drag and release which will show to the player how the single control works. There may be a text or visual prompt as a hint to show the player what to do.

Mockups

Other

This idea is based on a game I made for a game jam last year for the Computer Science House's "Devcade" which is an arcade machine they built. I originally built it in Monogame and want to tweak a lot of things compared to the original and have other ideas too.
